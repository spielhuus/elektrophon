elektrophon

analog and digital modules for an electroacustic instrument.

elektrophon is a collection of modular synthesizer modules i built. The module folders contain the schema and PCB files for kicad. You can find all the required files for changing the schemas and building gerber files in this project. is a collection of modules for a modular analog electro acoustic noise machine. all modules are built on stripboards or pcbâ€™s and with 3d printed front plates.The goal is it to build the modules quickly.

construction

the modules are mounted on eurorack rails. the side panels are made of wood. in my opinnion this is the simples method to create a complete rack. at the moment my rack is sufficent, but i believe it will soon be to small.

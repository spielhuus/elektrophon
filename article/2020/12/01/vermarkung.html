construction and measures of a synth rack.

when modules are built, they have to be mounted somewhere. the de facto standard is the eurorack format. the eurorack standard is derived from the 19-inch rack standard. this standard defines all the parts needed to build a complete rack. i am only using the rails to mount the modules. the rails will be screwed to a wooden side panel. you will read about that in a later article. in this article the pcb and panel sizes are described.

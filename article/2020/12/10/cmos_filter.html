low pass filter

lets get started with the simplest filter, a low pass filter.

Resistor Rload

for this filter we expect the cutoff frequency to be:

\[\begin{array}{c} f = \frac{1}{2 \pi RC} = \frac{1}{2 \pi 100k * 100n} = 159Hz \end{array}\]
